Question 17

Select the answer that correctly completes the sentence.

is responsible for identifying which business rules must be applied to the data of a company

Explanation: Governance rules and Business rules are different. Governance rules focus on access control to resources and datasets, while Business rules pertain to the specific rules governing the solution. A data analyst is primarily responsible for interpreting and analyzing data in the context of business rules.
Data engineers design and maintain the infrastructure for data, but typically do not handle business rules.

Correct Answer: A data analyst
