Question 9

What is a characteristic of a non-relational database?




Explanation:

Non-relational databases, often referred to as NoSQL databases, store data in flexible, self-describing structures such as JSON documents. This flexibility allows them to handle a variety of data types and formats, and they do not require a fixed schema.
