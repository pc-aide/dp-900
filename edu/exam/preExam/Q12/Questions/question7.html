Question 7

A retail point of sale (POS) system is an example of which type of solution?









Explanation:

An OLTP system is designed to handle real-time transactional operations, such as capturing and processing sales transactions, inventory management, and customer interactions. POS systems are typically used in retail environments to facilitate the sale of products or services to customers.

References:

  • OLTP and OLAP Overview

Correct Answer:
D. online transaction processing (OLTP)
