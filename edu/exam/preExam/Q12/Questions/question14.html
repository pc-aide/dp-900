Explanation:

Dimensions: In a star schema, the entities by which you aggregate or slice measures are called dimensions. Dimensions are descriptive attributes or perspectives of the data that provide context for the measures (also known as facts). For example, in a sales data star schema, the dimensions could be time, product, store location, and customer.
