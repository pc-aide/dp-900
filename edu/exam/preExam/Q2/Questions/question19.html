Why did sales increase last month?
Provide specific guidance on how to allocate current budget to buy different inventory items.
Detect images of the company's logo included in PDF documents.

Explanation:

1. Diagnostic Analytics: At this stage you can begin to answer some of those why questions. Historical data can begin to be measured against other data to answer the question of why something happened in the past. This is the process of gathering and interpreting different data sets to identify anomalies, detect patters, and determine relationships.

2. Prescriptive analytics is a combination of data, mathematical models, and various business rules to infer actions to influence future desired outcomes. Predictive analytics, broadly speaking, is a category of business intelligence that uses descriptive and predictive variables from the past to analyze and identify the likelihood of an unknown future outcome.

3. Cognitive: Involves detecting and interpreting data, such as images and text, using advanced technologies.
