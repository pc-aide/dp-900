Question 16

Match the types of workloads to the appropriate scenarios.

To answer, drag the appropriate workload type from the column on the left to its scenario on the right. Each workload type may be used once, more than once, or not at all.

Workload Types:

Batch
Streaming

Scenarios:

Explanation:

  • Batch: Used for analyzing historical data and classifying images that were collected or uploaded in the past.
  • Streaming: Used for tracking real-time data, such as how many people are currently using a website.

Correct Answers:

  • Analyzing historical data containing web traffic collected during the previous year: Batch
  • Classifying images that were uploaded last month: Batch
  • Tracking in real time how many people are currently using a website: Streaming
