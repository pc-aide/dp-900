Question 14

What is a characteristic of non-relational data?




Explanation:

Self-describing entities. This means that each entity, such as a document or a graph node, contains all the information necessary to describe itself and its attributes, without relying on a predefined schema or structure. This makes non-relational data more flexible and adaptable to different types of data and queries.
