Question 6

For each of the following statements, select Yes if the statement is true. Otherwise, select No.

Processing salary payments once a month is an example of a batch workload:



A wind turbine that sends 50 sensor readings per second is an example of a streaming workload:



A home electricity meter that sends readings once a day to an energy provider is an example of a streaming workload:



Explanation:

Streaming is synonymous with low latency (usually fractions of a second). A 24-hour interval does not constitute streaming in this scenario.

For the home electricity meter, it does not send every change immediately but gives daily data, which might contain just one reading or several readings at specific intervals.

References:

  • Batch Processing vs Stream Processing - Atlan

Correct Answers:
Processing salary payments once a month is an example of a batch workload: Yes
A wind turbine that sends 50 sensor readings per second is an example of a streaming workload: Yes
A home electricity meter that sends readings once a day to an energy provider is an example of a streaming workload: No
