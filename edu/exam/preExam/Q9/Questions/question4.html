Question 4

Which scenario is an example of a streaming workload?









Explanation:

A streaming workload typically involves the continuous and real-time ingestion and processing of data as it is generated. In the given scenario, sending telemetry data from edge devices involves the continuous flow of data from these devices to a central system for immediate analysis and processing. This aligns with the characteristics of a streaming workload where data is ingested in real-time and processed as it arrives.

Correct Answer:
C. sending telemetry data from edge devices
