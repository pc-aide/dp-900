Question 01

You have data saved in the following format:

<Person Age="23">
  <FirstName>John</FirstName>
  <LastName>Smith</LastName>
  <Hobbies>
    <Hobby Type="Sports">Golf</Hobby>
    <Hobby Type="Leisure">Reading</Hobby>
    <Hobby Type="Leisure">Guitar</Hobby>
  </Hobbies>
</Person>

Which format was used?

Explanation:

XML starts with a tag like <tag> and ends with a closing tag like </tag>, making it easy to identify.
